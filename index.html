About

Welcome to Spica Launch Co

Spica Launch Co is a team of passionate aerospace engineers committed to pushing the boundaries of rocket technology. Our mission is to create safe, efficient, and affordable rockets that will take us to new heights in space exploration.

We are dedicated to developing innovative solutions to the challenges of space travel, and our team is composed of talented engineers, designers, and visionaries who share a common goal. We are proud to be at the forefront of the next generation of space travel, and we are excited to see what the future holds.
